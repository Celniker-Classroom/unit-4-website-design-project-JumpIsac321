Marcus's Physics Website

Welcome! This is my physics website where I give a basic explanation of different areas of physics. Physics can defined as the scientific study of how objects change over time and interact with each other, and predicting how they will change in the future.

I enjoy physics because there are many different topics to learn about, like I show here, and you can always learn more about each subject. I hope this website functions as a starting point so that you can decide what you like and continue learning about it, and I hope you will share in my enjoyment of physics.

Classical mechanics

Classical mechanics is the basics of physics that applies in more real world situations, and it deals with objects under the influence of forces. Classical Mechanics

Electromagnetism

Electromagnetism is a branch of physics that deals with electric charges, the electric field, the magnetic field, and how they interact. Electromagnetism

Relativity

Relativity deals with objects that are moving near light speed, and also describes the curvature of space time. Relativity

Quantum mechanics

Quantum mechanics deals with very small objects, like atoms and electrons. Quantum mechanics
